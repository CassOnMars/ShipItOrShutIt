Manifesto for Startups and Side Projects

We are uncovering better ways of building
products by doing it and helping others do it.
Through this work we have come to value:

Users and Feedback over envisioning an ideal user
Shipping Quickly over long plans and maintaining perfection
Trial and Iteration over waiting to launch
Building and Creating over dreaming

That is, while there is value in the items on
the right, we value the items on the left more.

Ship It, or Shut It.
